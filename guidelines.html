COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

To keep our site safe and fun for everyone, please follow these rules:

GENERAL RULES

  • No spamming or flooding the posts or comments.
  • Be respectful to all users — no harassment, hate speech, or bullying.
  • No sharing of personal or private information.
  • Don't impersonate admins or other users.

POSTING & COMMENTING

  • Posts should be meaningful — avoid empty or repeated content.
  • Keep language appropriate for all ages.
  • Admins can remove inappropriate content without warning.

ADMINS

  • Admins are responsible for keeping the platform safe and clean.
  • Misuse of admin privileges can result in removal.
